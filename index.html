About Me

I'm Vitor Ângelis, a Graphic Desginer. Creativity and art have always been part of my life. Since I was a kid, drawing was a hobby (and I confess I was not that good). But I always liked to express my feelings and thoughts using a pencil or music. By the way, I love to work while I'm listening to good music because it's a powerful weapon to increase creativity.

I decided to study Graphic Design to put my mind and my abilities together. Since then, I've been working with awesome companies and professionals as a freelance designer.

Making a Logo is an extraordinary way to bring a brand to life. The visual, the colors, and the personality I can give to something is my work and my passion. To create something from a thought is a challenge that I'm ready for. Let's be creative?!
